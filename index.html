About Us

Burooj Group of Companies, based in Srinagar, Kashmir is business firm who has been dealing with electric vehicles. Recently, we acquired Master Distribution of Tesla Power USA. Tesla Power USA are the manufacturers of batteries, home & industrial UPS’s, solar power systems, water purifiers, FRC and fire extinguishers. They are also planning to launch a range of lubricants soon.

We, Burooj, are committed to bring a range of goods & services to the people of Jammu & Kashmir. We are currently dealing with Tesla Power USA products and services. We further plan to diversify the range of products & services, which include fitness & sports products, tour & travels and further more.

About Tesla Power USA

Tesla Power USA based in Gurugram, India, has been acknowledged for being a pioneer and leader in introducing affordable batteries with long life that has revolutionized the energy storage industry. The company has introduced first ever EV 2Wheelers with Fire Suppression Technology inside. With their expertise and years of research in “Healthy Water”, they have introduced a range of world-class Alkaline Water Purifiers. With a strong force of service engineers on the ground and largest service network, they provide excellent after sales services to the customers. They also offer extended life with warranty on used batteries, with their patented technology, ensuring that the customers save money and contribute in protecting the environment. The battery rejuvenation is the first registered brand “RESTORE” in India.
